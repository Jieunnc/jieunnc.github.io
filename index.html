Research Interests

Cerebrovascular & Lymphatic Biology
Investigating the role of cervical lymphatics in cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) drainage, adrenergic regulation, and ageing-related changes in brain fluid dynamics. Also studying vascular responses in photothrombotic ischemic stroke models using intravital imaging.
[Nature '25] [Nature Under Review] [Nat. Microbiology Submitted]
Quantitative Biomedical Image Analysis & Deep Learning
Developing deep learning models for quantitative analysis of biological imaging data, including vector field representation of red blood cell (RBC) flow from intravital microscopy, image denoising, and unpaired image-to-image translation preserving spatial and quantitative information.
[Cell Rep. Methods '25] [PhotoniX '25] [Nat. Methods '23]
Intravital Microscopy & In Vivo Imaging
Applying two-photon and intravital imaging techniques to visualize dynamic biological processes in living systems, including kidney pathology and nanomedicine delivery.
[BOE '23] [Adv. Ther. '25] [ACS AMI '23]
